## What we build

---

## Projects

### 🤖 [agent-company](https://github.com/waimagroup/agent-company)
A framework for designing, deploying, and orchestrating autonomous AI agents in production environments. Built around composable agent roles, structured task execution, and integration with MCP (Model Context Protocol) governance — so you get the power of agentic workflows without losing control of your systems.

---

### 📦 [ypm](https://github.com/waimagroup/ypm)
A lightweight project and dependency management utility tailored to the way Waima teams work. Cuts the overhead of scaffolding and configuration so engineers spend less time wiring things up and more time shipping.

---

### ⚡ [boostworker](https://github.com/waimagroup/boostworker)
A background job and task-processing worker designed for high-throughput workloads. Handles queue management, retry logic, and concurrency so your core application stays lean and your pipelines stay reliable.

---

### 🥷 [ninjachallenge](https://github.com/waimagroup/ninjachallenge)
A gamified technical challenge platform used to assess developer skills through real-world engineering problems. Designed to make hiring evaluation more signal-rich and less ceremony-heavy.

---

### 🏙️ [bookthecity](https://github.com/waimagroup/bookthecity)
A booking and discovery platform connecting users with urban experiences, venues, and services across cities. Built for speed, mobile-first, and designed to scale across markets.
